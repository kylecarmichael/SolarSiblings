Jupiter

Fast Facts

The 5th planet from the sun

Naming

As the largest planet, it is named after the king of the Roman gods.

Saturn

Fast Facts

The 6th planet from the sun

Naming

This planet is named after the Roman god of agriculture and wealth, who is the father of Jupiter.

Uranus

Fast Facts

The 7th planet from the sun

Naming

This name only gained accepted use in the 1800s after being established by German astronomer Johann Elert Bode. Uranus is the father of Saturn in mythology.

Neptune

Fast Facts

The 8th and final planet from the sun

Naming

This planet was mathematicaly calculated by French astronomer Le Verrier and discover in 1846. The planet is named after the Roman god of the sea.
